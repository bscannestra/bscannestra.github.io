ABOUT REPORTING NARRATIVE

My name is B. Sakura Cannestra, and welcome to my website!

I’m currently a second-year graduate student at the UC Berkeley Graduate School of Journalism, focused on web design/development, writing passionately, and telling interactive stories.

Previously, I have worked at POLITICO California as an intern, covering tech regulation and policy in California and helping to organize the California Playbook newsletter.

I graduated from UC Berkeley in 2020 with a bachelor’s degree in English, minors in History and Creative Writing. In that time, I also grew my skills at The Daily Californian, covering everything from student government to higher education policy to protests attended by thousands. I also have experience in writing personal essay-style columns and publishing illustrations, also from The Daily Californian.

My interests in journalism are wide and varied, though my primary topic of interests are tech — tech culture, policy, innovation and advocacy. I am also very interested in new forms of telling stories, such as building digital interactive packages or audience engagement on social media.

I'm an aspiring plant dad and an avid calligrapher with a small collection of gel pens.
