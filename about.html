About Clips Creative

Hi there! My name is B. Sakura Cannestra and I am an aspiring journalist and creative writer based in San Jose, CA.

I graduated in the class of 2020 from UC Berkeley with a Bachelor’s Degree in English and minors in History and Creative writing. While in college, I worked for The Daily Californian newspaper for four years, where I wrote about topics ranging from student lobbying to the Berkeley city government to protests. I particularly loved stories on governmental processes, ranging from the student government all the way to national topics! Besides reporting, I also love researching how the past impacts today. Check out my journalism Clips here.

The word “story” has another insinuation, too — I love writing short fiction and poetry, and I hope to write and illustrate my own webcomic one day. I've always had a passion for creative writing, and I'd love for you to read some of my pieces. Check out my Creative tab for more.

Feel free to reach out — you can contact me at bearcannestra@gmail.com!
