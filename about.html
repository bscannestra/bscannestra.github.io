About Clips

B. Sakura Cannestra is an aspiring journalist and creative writer who loves pursing a good story.

She graduated in the class of 2020 from UC Berkeley with a Bachelor’s Degree in English and minors in History and Creative writing. While in college, she worked for The Daily Californian for four years, where she wrote about topics ranging from student lobbying to the Berkeley city government, from protests to weekly meetings. She has a passion for writing about bureaucracy on the local and state levels, regularly deciphering California legislation for stories. Besides reporting on the current events, Sakura also enjoys researching how history has impacted modern life. Check out the Clips tab to see some articles she’s written.

The word “story” has another insinuation, too — Sakura loves writing short fiction and poetry, and even hopes to create her own webcomic one day. Check out the Creative tab to see some of her creative writing.

Feel free to reach out — you can reach out to Sakura at bearcannestra@gmail.com!
